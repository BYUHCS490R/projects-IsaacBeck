Structural Tags

header, nav, main, footer

In the header goes different tabs within the website.

Nav contains the main links for your website. Nav can go in the header. It does not have to go into the header. It cannot contain links to another website.

In main goes the website's main content.

Inside the footer goes all of the extra stuff that does not need to be in main. It also has links.
