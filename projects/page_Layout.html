Ancient China

Ancient China has a very rich history and culture. China has had many dynasties like the Qin, Han, and Tang. The last dynasty was the Qing which ended in 1912.

China also has philosophy and religion. There is Taoism, Confucianism, and Buddism, which are called the three teachings. There is also chinese folk religion and ancestor worship.
