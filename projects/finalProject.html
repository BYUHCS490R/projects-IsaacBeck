Asian Food

I have lived in China for several years, and as a result, I have eaten many different types of food from various Asian countries and cultures. While living there, I have found some great food that I want to share and recommend with you. These dishes taste amazing and have unique and interesting flavors.

This website shares my favorite foods from China, Japan, South Korea, Vietnam, and India. It also explains how they are made. “Asian cuisine emphasizes a combination of flavors and textures, often within a single dish.” (Asian Nation: Asian Food)
