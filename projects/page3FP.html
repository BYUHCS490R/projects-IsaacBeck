Bibimbap (South Korea)

Bibimbap

Bibimbap is a South Korean dish made up of a bowl of rice with vegetables, meat, an egg, and gochujang (red chili) sauce. I like it because of how it combines multiple flavors and textures into a single dish, making it very good to eat. It is interesting because it is eaten while very hot in a heated stone bowl.

Bibimbap is made by getting rice in a bowl, and adding on top of it vegetables, meat, an egg, and gouchjang sauce. Everything is then mixed together to eat.
