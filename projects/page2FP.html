Sushi (Japan)

Sushi

Sushi is type of Japanese food made from rice combined with raw or uncooked fish, seafood, and vegetables, then wrapped in seaweed. I really like sushi because of its flavor and taste, and for how many different varieties of it there are. Some of these are nigiri (rice with fish on top) and sashimi (just fish by itself).

To make sushi, you just take rice (with vinegar, sugar, and salt), and shape it over seaweed, then add fish and vegetables. After that you simply roll it up and cut it into pieces. Sushi can also be eaten with soy sauce and other sauces.
