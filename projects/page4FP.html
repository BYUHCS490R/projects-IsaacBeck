Pho (Vietnam)

Pho

Pho is a Vietnamese noodle soup made with rice noodles, broth, herbs, and meat (usually beef). Pho is one of my favorite foods because of how the flavor of the meat combines with the herbs and the noodles. Each pho is different because “One person's phở will not taste like another's because of upbringing, individual tastes, availability of ingredients, etc.” (The Spruce Eats: Vietnamese Pho Recipe)

You make pho just by simmering the meat with spices to make the broth, and then adding the rice noodles and herbs. It can be made in a variety of ways because of which ingredients and herbs you decide to use, and depending on how you want it prepared.
