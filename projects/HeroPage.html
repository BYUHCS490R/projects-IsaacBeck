Hero

Yao Ming was a Chinese basketball player who played in the 2008 Olympics. He has also won the FIBA Asia Cup in 2001, 2003, and 2005, becoming MVP all three times. He retired in 2011 because of injuries.

#1
Yao Ming is 7 ft 6 in height.
#2
Yao Ming was the chairman of the Chinese Basketball Association from 2017-2024.
#3
In 2016 Yao Ming was elected into the Naismith Basketball Hall of Fame.
